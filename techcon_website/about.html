Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the years, we've grown into one of the premier technology conferences worldwide, with our 2023 event hosting over 10,000 attendees across three days of innovation and learning.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. We bring together thought leaders, developers, and business executives to foster collaboration and accelerate technological adoption across industries.

Our core principles:

  • Democratizing access to tech education
  • Showcasing diverse voices in technology
  • Creating meaningful networking opportunities
